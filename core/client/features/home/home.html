The nautilus uses its jet to propel itself backwards through deep ocean waters.

It can’t see where it’s going.

Unlike the nautilus, you know where you’re going. Hawaii. Europe. The Caribbean.

But, when it comes to travel planning, sometimes the nautilus is right: going backwards makes more sense.

SkyNautilus allows you to search for flights to your desired destination, while keeping your options for your departure city open. It’s possible that you can find cheaper fares by planning each leg of your trip backwards.

Say you live in Salt Lake City and you want to fly to London. With SkyNautilus, you can plan your layovers. Now you can take advantage of low fares on different airlines, or crash on your friend's couch in another city in order to catch that cheap flight the next morning.

Start by searching for flights to London. SkyNautilus will return results that include different possible cities of origin. Find the cheapest option, say Orlando. Now, plan how you'll get to Orlando.
